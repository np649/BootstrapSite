Scuderia Toro Rosso

Scuderia Toro Rosso is the Red Bull Racing junior team, meaning that the car they race is a derivative of the Red Bull and the Toro Rosso drivers are all under Red Bull contracts. Toro Rosso has always been towards the back of the grid, sadly never having a very competitive package. Although with the upcoming budget cap for teams Toro Rosso is hoping to become more competitive.
