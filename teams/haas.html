Haas F1 Team

Being the newest team in F1 Haas does not have much of a history in the sport. Joining the grid in 2016 the team has been steadily improving their performance season after season. Based in North Carolina, Haas is the only American F1 team on the grid. The team has garnered some controversy due to how they purchase a large number of their car's parts directly from Ferrari.
