BWT Racing Point F1 Team

Racing Point up until last season was known as Force India Racing Team, but when they went bankrupt the team was sold to a consortium of investors led by Lawrence Stroll, father of F1 driver Lance Stroll. The Racing Point F1 Team has been stuck in the midfield for a long time struggling to find any improvements in their chassis design. Hopefully with the technical changes coming to the sport in 2022 the team can challenge for the championship.
