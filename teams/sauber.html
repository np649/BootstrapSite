Alfa Romeo Sauber Motorsport AG

Sauber has been in Formula One since the late 1990's and has been both extremely competitive and incredibly slow. Unfortunately for Sauber they are currently the latter, struggling to consistently score points. Although with their Ferrari engines rumoured to be getting a big upgrade we may see that change in the near future.
