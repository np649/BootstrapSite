Renault F1 Team

The Renault F1 Team is a somewhat new addition to grid only arriving to the sport in 2000, winning the 2005 and 2006 championship. Technically they began competing in 1977 but withdrew from the sport in 1985 due to rising costs in the sport. Although Renault as an F1 team has been on and off the grid for years, they have existed as an engine manufacturer since the early days of the sport. In fact the Renault F1 Team and Renault Racing Engines exist as two separate entities in two different countries. The Renault F1 Team have been strong players in the midfield of the grid, meaning that they usually finish around P6 to P12.
