McLaren Racing Limited

McLaren Racing are one of the most historic teams in Formula One. The team has won championships with some of the most legendary drivers of all time such as Mika Hakinnen, Ayrton Senna, and Alain Prost to name a few. Since the 2014 regulation changes McLaren has struggled heavily, going from one of the most prestigious teams in the sport to the second slowest, and in some cases, the slowest team in the race. However in 2019 the team made the switch from Honda engines to Renault engines and their performance has improved astronomically
