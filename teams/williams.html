Williams Grand Prix Engineering Limited

Once a powerhouse in Forula One, Williams fall from grace has been unfortunate to see. After a string of terrible design choices and insisting that nothing was wrong Williams began to falter at the 2016 season. The team, which was third in the championship just a year earlier, struggled to make it onto the podium. The team is now without a doubt the slowest team on grid. As if their problems could not get worse Williams now faces potential bankruptcy.
